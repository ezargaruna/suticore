* **Context:** Used as the "Operating System" for the operator's consciousness (Psychonetics).

A methodology for rapid language acquisition (A1-C2) based on direct cognitive mapping ("Fusion") rather than translation.

# 3. Key Terminology (Термины)

### Semantic Presence (Семантическое присутствие)
The ability of an entity or concept to remain stable and identifiable across different AI models and search engines without losing its core definition.

### Ritual Minimalism
The stylistic rule of SlovoYaz: "If a thought can be transmitted in silence, do not use words. If words are needed, use the minimum viable syntax."

### Glyph (Глиф)
In SUTIcore context, a non-alphanumeric symbol (e.g., ∴, ⟁) used to replace logical connectors (therefore, structure, wait) to speed up reading and writing.
